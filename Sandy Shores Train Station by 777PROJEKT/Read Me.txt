

Sandy Shores Train Station by 777PROJEKT


Waiting for a Sandy Shores train station, i do my own... but i'm glad to see others versions :)


You need:

Map Editor : https://www.gta5-mods.com/scripts/map-editor 
            
Object Spawn Unlocker or Menyoo :  https://www.gta5-mods.com/scripts/object-spawn-unlocker
                                :  https://www.gta5-mods.com/scripts/menyoo-pc-sp

Object list 10k : https://www.gta5-mods.com/tools/object-list-10k




There are 2 files.xml with different atmospheres, one for the day and one for the night.

Put the 2 files in Gta V main filder, in game open Map Editor and load "t day" or "t night"




I don't tested with all trains but it's ok for Siemens Desiro : https://fr.gta5-mods.com/vehicles/siemens-desiro-rus-lastochka
                                          and Intercity Wagon : https://fr.gta5-mods.com/vehicles/db-intercity-wagon
